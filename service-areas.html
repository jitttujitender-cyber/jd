About Our Coverage

Why We Cover Northwest Nebraska

Northwest Nebraska has a unique mix of older homes, rural properties, and small commercial buildings that require plumbing professionals who understand local conditions. Harsh winters can freeze exposed pipes, hard well water accelerates mineral buildup in fixtures and water heaters, and many properties run on septic systems that need regular attention.

Tom Carter Drain & Plumbing was built to serve this region specifically. We are not a franchise dispatching strangers — we are a local operation that knows Chadron and its neighboring towns because we live and work here. That local knowledge makes a real difference when you need fast, accurate plumbing help.
