Safety-First Gas Work

Professional Gas Line Repair You Can Trust

Gas line issues are not a job for guesswork. Whether you have a confirmed leak, a suspected connection problem, or you need a new line run for an appliance, the work needs to be done correctly the first time. Gas line repair and service in Chadron, NE addresses leaks, deteriorating connections, corroded fittings, and damaged line sections with proper pressure testing before and after every repair.

We also extend gas lines for new appliances — ranges, dryers, outdoor grills, fire pits, standby generators, and water heaters — and handle gas shut-off valve replacements when an older valve has become unreliable. Every project closes with a pressure test and a review of the work completed so you understand exactly what was done and can feel confident that your gas system is safe and reliable.

If you smell gas: leave immediately, do not switch any lights or appliances on or off, and call 911. Then call us once you are safely away from the building.
