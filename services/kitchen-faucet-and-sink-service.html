Faucet Repair, Replacement & Sink Service

A dripping kitchen faucet may seem minor, but a faucet dripping once per second wastes over 3,000 gallons of water per year. Kitchen faucet and sink service in Chadron, NE addresses leaking faucets, worn cartridges, faulty shutoff valves, corroded supply lines, slow or clogged sink drains, and new faucet installations — whether you want a repair on an existing fixture or a brand new one installed.

We service all faucet types including single-handle, double-handle, pull-down, and commercial-style faucets. If you are upgrading your kitchen and want a new faucet installed as part of the project, we handle the supply line connections, drain hookups, and garbage disposal tie-ins to make sure everything works together correctly from the start.
