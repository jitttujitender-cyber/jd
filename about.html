About Tom Carter Drain & Plumbing

Local plumbing and drain support built on clear communication, careful work, and community trust.

Who We Are

Reliable Help for Chadron Homes & Businesses

Tom Carter Drain & Plumbing helps homeowners, small businesses, landlords, and property managers solve plumbing and drain issues with less stress. From emergency drain cleaning to water heater repair and routine fixture service, we focus on doing the job right and leaving your space clean. Our approach is simple: show up on time, explain the problem honestly, and complete the work with care.

We serve Chadron and surrounding northwest Nebraska communities, and we understand the unique needs of properties in this region. Older homes with aging pipes, seasonal freeze risks, and hard water conditions all require someone with local knowledge and a practical mindset. That is exactly what we bring to every job.

Our Service Area

Proudly Serving Chadron & Northwest Nebraska

We are based in Chadron and serve communities across a wide stretch of northwest Nebraska, including Alliance, Scottsbluff, Gering, Crawford, Hay Springs, Gordon, Rushville, Hemingford, Harrison, Mitchell, and O'Neill. Our familiarity with the region's housing stock, water systems, and seasonal conditions allows us to provide targeted solutions that actually fit the problem.

Whether you are a homeowner dealing with a sudden leak, a landlord managing a rental property, or a business owner who needs commercial plumbing maintenance, we bring the same level of care and professionalism to every call. Our goal is to be the plumber you trust and recommend to your neighbors.
